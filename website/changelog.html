Changelog

TermQuarium just had its first tracked release — this page will grow with every one after it.

v1.0.0 Initial release
Added
  • Four species — Goldfish, Angelfish, Betta, and the predatory Shark
  • Six personalities (Friendly, Explorer, Shy, Greedy, Lazy, Playful) plus an independent Sleepy trait
  • Earned relationships — a private bond score per pair, remembering up to 5 reasons why
  • Container homes — the Rock and Castle, with real sleeping capacity fish queue for by preference
  • A continuous day/night cycle with occasional morning wake-up vignettes
  • Breeding — eligible bonded pairs can have babies, capped so the tank never overruns
  • Visitor economy, Shop, and an Emergency Aquarium Welfare safety net for a bankrupt tank
  • Named saves with rename/duplicate/delete, and a pause that actually freezes the simulation
  • Standalone Windows installer
